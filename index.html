Module One: The 18-week pre-block course…
 

Learners complete the Student Activity Workbook in your own time. This is a 60-page hard copy workbook to be completed over an 18-week period prior to the 5-week block course. On average the workbook activity requires 3-4 pages per week. Reference material provided includes the Safety in Small Craft book.
 

Learners are fully supported by our tutorial staff during this phase of the course. You can call, text or email at any time!
 

Module Two: The 5-week block course...
 

The block course takes place in our classroom and onboard a working vessel for certain activities, e.g. radar. During the block course students complete the required Maritime NZ Training Record Book (TRB) under the guidance of our tutors and support staff.  Theory and practical tasks are assessed and signed off in preparation for the Skipper Restricted Licence oral exam. This is conducted by an external Maritime NZ examiner on the last day of the course.
